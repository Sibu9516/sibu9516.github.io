## ❓ Frequently asked questions

How do I add a new database driver?
Open the Database settings menu and select the Data Sources link. Click the plus sign, select your driver type, and click the Download link if the application prompts for missing driver files.

Can I run multiple instances of the software?
Yes, you can open several project windows at the same time. Each window operates independently of the others.

Is my data stored on the cloud?
No, the application stores your local connection settings and project files on your physical machine. You maintain full control over your data storage.

What should I do if the software does not connect to the server?
Check your internet connection first. Then, verify that your firewall allows traffic on the port used by your database service. Confirm your credentials are correct by trying to log into the database using a different tool.
